How to Play - 🎮

  • Move Agent 🕵️ : Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move around and dodge Villains.
  • Shoot Villains 🦹 : Press Space to fire bullets.
  • Collect Martinis🍸 : Find martinis for extra lives.
  • Level 🆙 : As you progress the level increases, bringing tougher enemies.

Your Objective

Eliminate as many Bond Villains as you can and survive as long as possible to get a high score. Good luck Mr. Bond
